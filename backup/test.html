The Modern Habitat

We live as rewarded animals in an economy of attention. Every notification, scroll, and click activates ancient survival circuits, now endlessly looped by algorithms. This environment conditions us for distraction, leaving us feeling burned out and unfocused.

Project Dukkha provides the map and compass to navigate this terrain. It's not about escaping technology, but mastering your internal response to it through evidence-based protocols.
