The Modern Habitat

We are rewarded animals living in an economy of attention. Every notification, scroll, and click is engineered to trigger ancient survival circuits, now endlessly looped by algorithms. This environment conditions us for distraction, leaving us feeling burned out and unfocused.

Project Dukkha provides the map and compass to navigate this terrain. It's not about escaping technology, but mastering your internal response to it.
