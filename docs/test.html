The Modern Habitat

We are rewarded animals living in an economy of attention. Every notification, scroll, and click is engineered to trigger ancient survival circuits, now endlessly looped by algorithms designed for engagement over wellbeing.

This environment conditions us for distraction, leaving us feeling burned out, unfocused, and disconnected from our deeper motivations. Project Dukkha provides the map and compass to navigate this terrain—not by escaping technology, but by mastering your internal response to it.
